More About Us

Our Services.

To succeed and sustain we need an innovative approach for which we work closely with Client/Consultants to adopt smart solutions. Below are the services we are expertised in...

MEP Services.

MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) services play a major role in operations. WeCare provide Integrated MEP services providing expert solutions in the building, industrial and infrastructure segments.

Cleaning Services.

Housekeeping is not limited to just cleanliness alone. It defines the 'Neatness and Orderliness' of work areas, tripping hazards, removal of wastages, debris, clutter & spills and other relevant Fire hazards, from home and work areas. WeCare has proven expertise in providing all type of cleaning services over wide range of industry verticals.

Turn Key Projects.

WeCare provides turn-key services for all of its projects commencing at concept and continuing beyond the project’s final punch list. Various project services ranging from Civil Works to HVAC will be handled by our expertise staff with decades of experience.

Office Support Services.

Our company is an established brand name for sourcing of talent for contract staff to support their basic administrative chores as well as office support. The Business Support Services Wing, undertakes talent search for 'right personnel for the right job', based on the candidates performance at the aptitude test and interview.

Guest House Management.

WeCare Facility Services trained the staff for proper maintenance of Guest House. All Staff members are trained in handling of care-taking, housekeeping and pantry services for comfortable stay. This serve is performed by highly trained and professionals to ensure quality and customers satisfaction. Our staff is placed 24 hours a day for daily cleaning. Thus we have emerged as a one stop solution to all of cleaning requirements and management services.

Who Are We.

'We Care Facility Services’ is one stop solution for Facilities and Project Management Services established in 2014 by like minded entrepreneurs. We are equipped with 400 ground staff, serving customers across various segments such as IT/ITES, Banking, Retail, Health Care, Hospitality and Govt sector.

Our Process.

There would be a site SURVEY done by our expert team before the work commencement to understand all the drawings and getting the approvals from the Client/Consultant. Our expert team will be tracking each activity from Day 1. All the Works are supervised by senior management constantly at all levels to achieve the right quality. The Site is then Monitored as per the organisation matrix of WeCare constantly, and make sure there is no errors at the work location.

Quality.

Quality for WeCare means, using standardised material with Quality Tools and Training Team, through which we can achieve quality. Regular inspections are carried out for machinery and tools to ensure there are no breakdowns.
